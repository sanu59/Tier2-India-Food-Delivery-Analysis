# Tier 2 India Food Delivery Analysis

## Overview
Analysed 43,307 restaurants across 14 Tier 2 Indian cities 
using Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Plotly and Machine Learning 
to uncover food delivery market gaps and business opportunities.

## Key Findings
- Visakhapatnam is the best value food city in Tier 2 India
  — fastest delivery (20.6 min), good ratings, lowest prices
- Price has near zero correlation (0.01) with restaurant quality
  — cheap restaurants rate as well as expensive ones
- Vadodara has only 6.28% South Indian restaurants despite 
  being a large Tier 2 city — biggest market gap identified
- Jaipur has the highest average restaurant rating (3.99)
- Built a Gradient Boosting ML model to predict restaurant 
  success — 57% accuracy revealing that operational metrics 
  alone cannot predict success

## Business Recommendations
- Zomato should prioritise Visakhapatnam for premium partnerships
- South Indian chains should expand to Vadodara and Nagpur first
- New restaurants need not invest in premium pricing to get 
  high ratings — quality matters more than price
